Honey Butter Cornbread

Description

Indulge in the rich and comforting flavors of Honey Butter Cornbread. This delightful recipe elevates the classic cornbread experience by incorporating the sweetness of honey and the decadence of butter. Each bite encapsulates a perfect balance of moist texture and golden sweetness, making it a true crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, honey, milk, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Transfer the batter to the prepared baking pan and bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cornbread to cool slightly before slicing and serving.
