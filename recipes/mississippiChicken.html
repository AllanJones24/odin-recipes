Mississippi Chicken

Description

Mississippi Chicken is a culinary delight that effortlessly combines simplicity with crowd-pleasing flavors, making it a standout dish for any occasion. This delectable chicken recipe captures the essence of Southern comfort cuisine, bringing together a medley of savory and tangy notes that dance on the taste buds. The dish typically features succulent chicken pieces, seasoned to perfection and slow-cooked to achieve a tender, melt-in-your-mouth texture. What sets Mississippi Chicken apart is the unique twist it takes with the addition of pepperoncini peppers and their briny, slightly spicy kick. These peppers infuse the dish with a depth of flavor that is both unexpected and utterly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 8 pepperoncini peppers

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in the bottom of a slow cooker.
  2. Sprinkle ranch seasoning and au jus mix over the chicken.
  3. Top with butter and pepperoncini peppers.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  5. Shred chicken with two forks and serve over mashed potatoes or rice.
